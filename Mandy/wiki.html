Boings Nightmare Aircraft

The 737 was the most successfull aircraft manufactured in Boingś history. The beginning of the nightmare has its roots in the rivalry between Airbus and Boing. For Airbus the flagship of the company is the Airbus 320 wheras Boing tries to cover the same Market with their flagship the 737.When Airbus announced an updated version of there flagship the A320, Boing had no choice but measuring up with some innovation to their flagship too.

What happened

Fuel efficenicy is the keyword in the aviation world. So Airbus came up with an idea of a new fuel efficient version of their aircraft. All they did was using bigger engines. The aircrafts from Airbus are higher from the ground, so fitting the bigger engines underneath the wings was no problem for Airbus. Boing could not just copy the idea and fit bigger engines under their wings too, as the aircraft is lower. So they came up with another idea to fit the engines.

The idea to use software

Boing came up with the idea to use software to help them to solve any construction issues on their aircarft. Because the bigger engines could not fit under the wings, the whole aerodynamic of the aircraft was not function anymore. The aircraft nose pitched up and the software was supposed to regulate this.

How good was the idea to rely entirely on the software

The software was malfunctioning twice. The pilots were not informed about the functioning of the software and could not interfere with it. Two crashes and several victims later, the aircraft was grounded.
